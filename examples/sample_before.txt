INVESTIGATION REPORT - CONFIDENTIAL

Date: 14 March 2024
Case Officer: Detective Jan de Vries
Reference: 2024/INV/0847

SUMMARY OF EVENTS

On 16 October 2023, officers arrested Johannes van der Berg at Stationsplein 42, Rotterdam.
Van der Berg was found in possession of documentation linking him to Marcus Johnson,
a known associate operating from Brussels.

Financial records show transfers from Van der Berg's account (IBAN: NL91 ABNA 0417 1643 00)
to Meridian Holdings Ltd, totaling EUR 2.4 million between March and September 2023.

WITNESS STATEMENT

Maria Petrova (+31 6 12345678) confirmed she observed Van der Berg meeting with
Johnson at Hotel Krasnapolsky, Amsterdam on 3 separate occasions:
- 12 March 2023
- 28 May 2023
- 14 September 2023

Ms. Petrova's statement was corroborated by security footage and her colleague
Sophie Bakker (sophie.bakker@example.com), who was present at the May meeting.

VEHICLE INFORMATION

Van der Berg was driving a BMW X5 (license plate: XX-123-YY) registered to
his company address at Keizersgracht 221, Amsterdam.

Johnson arrived in a Mercedes E-Class, traced to a rental company in Antwerp.

RECOMMENDATIONS

Further investigation into Meridian Holdings Ltd recommended.
Company registered in Cyprus, beneficial owner unknown.
